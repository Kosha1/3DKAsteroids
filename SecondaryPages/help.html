Objective

Our command ship has been disabled after an enemy attack
Sweep the asteroid field and protect the ship from collsions. The ship will undergo critical failure if two asteroids strike it

Controls

Space: Fire

Steering

  • W: Pitch Up   S: Pitch Down
    A: Yaw Left   D: Yaw Right
    Left Arrow: Roll Counterclockwise   Right Arrow: Roll Clockwise

Speed

  • Up Arrow: Increase Speed   Down Arrow: Reduce Speed (can go down to a speed of 0)
    Top left corner of screen has an indicator of the current speed

Ship View

  • A view of the ship and dangerously close asteroids is provided in the bottom right
    Press X to change between a front view and top view of the ship
    Color represents depth. Red objects are closer to the camera and white objects are farther

Miscellaneous

  • The top right corner of screen has useful information in case you get disoriented
    The pitch and yaw values represent the angles to rotate in order to head towards the command ship
    Positive Yaw Angle: Rotate Right   Negative Yaw Angle: Rotate Left
    Positive Pitch Angle: Rotate Up   Negative Yaw Angle: Rotate Down
    Note: First get the yaw angle close to 0 and then look at the pitch. Changing the pitch angle first will also change the yaw angle

Warning!

This game heavily consumes GPU resources (and drains battery quickly). If your FPS is significantly less than 60 (or whatever your monitor's refresh rate is), the game will not be very playable
Try running it on a device with a better video card
